In the past four months of MDEF at IAAC in Barcelona I walked together with 28 classmates through eight courses. Since the last Design Studio reflections (2) the collaborative that initially started as 'Solarpunk' turned into a food waste workshop initiative. Parallel to this ran the Duckweed Incubator dual project and personal work.
