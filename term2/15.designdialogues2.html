Design Dialogues 2

On the 29th of March we exhibited term two's outcomes in the second Design Dialogues. With a similiar setup as the first edition we stalled our projects in IAAC's main hall.

In the week leading up to final presentation we were asked to deliver a 'in a nutshell'-overview of our projects by answering some key questions.

figure 1: Information sheet about the project's strategy.

Different than previous DD I found that all the projects displayed were more representatives of their creators and more context-specific adapted. We had more to show and it made more (meaningful) sense. Personally my main aims for the day were to get some in-depth comments on the technical aspects of the drone and ideate/speculate on future applications.
