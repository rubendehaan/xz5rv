Drone

Recently I came across the CircuitMess Wheelson on the Arduino website and got the idea to initiate a setup for a Arduino drone. Since there is plenty of information on the topic on numerous blogs, online communities and webshops, creating the concept of the hardware turned out not to be difficult. I ordered the first quarter of the EMC/rotor/motor assembly and tested it.
