Design studio 4

Just over halfway through the second term a review with Guillem was scheduled to discuss proceedings.

Project continuation

In the session I described the current statuses of the projects together with Guillem. Since his feedback on project Duckweed during the Design Dialogues was very valuable I was especially curious to hear his view on the past- and next steps.

figure 1: Prepared overview of ongoing topics.

  • "The kitchen is the most common workplace almost all people have available."
  • "Don't think in persona's."
  • "Think of it more as a long-term recipe; How many days does it have to grow to generate how many food?"
  • "The more radical you pick your approach, the more you will enjoy it."
  • "I really like the futuristic, specified POV displayed in the appliance visuals."
  • Maybe the mission is to hand New-Ruralists an up-to-date opportunity for them to create meaning through
    diversification. A modern Homo Universalis: The Post-Artisan.
